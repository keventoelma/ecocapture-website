Latest News – Empowering Farmers with Sustainable Solutions

EcoCapture Solutions is proud to announce the expansion of our farmer support program, delivering high-quality biochar-based fertilizers directly to farming communities across East Africa. By combining innovative CO₂ capture technology with advanced greenhouse solutions, we are helping farmers increase crop yields, improve soil health, and reduce environmental impact. Our recent rollout of 25kg biochar fertilizer sacks ensures affordability and accessibility, making sustainable farming a reality for more households. Together, we’re building a greener, more productive future for agriculture.
