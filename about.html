About Us

EcoCapture Solutions Ltd was founded with one mission: transform CO₂ from a climate threat into a growth engine for farmers and industries across Africa. Our locally built technology captures CO₂ efficiently, powers greenhouse farming, and supports sustainable fertilizer production — all at a fraction of global costs.

Our Vision

A carbon-neutral Africa where farmers thrive with climate-smart technology.

Our Mission

Provide affordable CO₂ capture solutions that create value for agriculture, industry, and the planet.
